About Me

I’m a PhD candidate in Astrophysics at UNAM and the University of Groningen. My work focuses on cosmology, gravitational lensing, and the application of deep learning to extract physical and cosmological information from large-scale surveys. I'm a member of the LSST collaboration and currently joining the Euclid mission. I’m passionate about using artificial intelligence to push the frontiers of modern astrophysics and science communication.

Projects

  • Master's Thesis: Optimization of cosmological systems using Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
  • LEAPS 2023 (ESA & Leiden): Evaluation of a neural network-based chemical emulator for hydrodynamic galaxy simulations
  • PhD Research: Reconstruction and inference of strong gravitational lenses using deep generative models

Publications
